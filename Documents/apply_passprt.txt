# How to Apply for a Passport in India (Online & Offline)

A **passport** is an official document issued by the **Government of India** that allows citizens to travel internationally. It also serves as a valid **proof of identity and citizenship**.

In India, passports are issued by the **Ministry of External Affairs (MEA)** through **Passport Seva Kendras (PSK)** and **Post Office Passport Seva Kendras (POPSK)**.

Applicants can apply for a passport **online through the Passport Seva Portal or offline at designated centres**.

Official Portal:
https://www.passportindia.gov.in/

---

# Eligibility Criteria for Passport Application

To apply for a passport in India, the applicant must:

* Be a **citizen of India**
* Be **18 years or older** to apply independently
* Minors can apply through their **parents or legal guardians**

### Passport Validity

* **Adults:** Passport validity is **10 years**
* **Minors:** Passport validity is **5 years** or until the age of 18

---

# How to Apply for Passport Online

Applicants can apply online through the **Passport Seva Portal**.

Official Portal:
https://www.passportindia.gov.in/

---

## Step-by-Step Process

**Step 1:** Visit the Passport Seva Portal:
https://www.passportindia.gov.in/

**Step 2:** Click **“Register Now”** to create an account.

**Step 3:** Log in using your **Login ID and password**.

**Step 4:** Click **“Apply for Fresh Passport / Re-issue of Passport.”**

**Step 5:** Fill out the online passport application form with required personal details.

**Step 6:** Submit the form.

**Step 7:** Go to **“View Saved/Submitted Applications.”**

**Step 8:** Click **“Pay and Schedule Appointment.”**

**Step 9:** Choose a **Passport Seva Kendra (PSK) or POPSK** and book an appointment.

**Step 10:** Pay the application fee online.

**Step 11:** Download and print the **application receipt**, which contains the **Application Reference Number (ARN)**.

On the appointment date, visit the selected **Passport Seva Kendra** with original documents for verification.

---

# How to Apply for Passport Offline

Applicants who prefer offline mode can apply through authorised passport centres.

These include:

* **Passport Seva Kendras (PSK)**
* **Post Office Passport Seva Kendras (POPSK)**
* **District Passport Cells (DPC)**
* **Common Service Centres (CSC)**

---

## Offline Application Process

**Step 1:** Obtain the **passport application form** from a passport centre or download it from the Passport Seva portal.

**Step 2:** Fill out the application form carefully.

**Step 3:** Attach required **self-attested documents and photographs**.

**Step 4:** Submit the form at the designated passport centre.

**Step 5:** Pay the applicable **passport application fee**.

**Step 6:** Collect the **acknowledgement slip with the file number** for tracking your application status.

---

# Documents Required for Passport Application

Applicants must submit valid documents for identity, address, and date of birth.

Commonly accepted documents include:

### Address Proof

* Aadhaar card
* Utility bills
* Rent agreement
* Voter ID card
* Bank account passbook

### Identity Proof

* Aadhaar card
* Voter ID
* PAN card
* Driving licence

### Date of Birth Proof

* Birth certificate
* School leaving certificate
* Aadhaar card
* Passport of parents (for minors)

Original documents must be presented during verification.

# Passport Application Fees

| Service                              | Fee             |
| ------------------------------------ | --------------- |
| Fresh passport (36 pages)            | ₹1,500          |
| Fresh passport (60 pages)            | ₹2,000          |
| Minor passport (below 18 years)      | ₹1,000          |
| Replacement of lost/damaged passport | ₹3,000 – ₹3,500 |
| Police Clearance Certificate (PCC)   | ₹500            |
| Tatkal service additional fee        | ₹2,000          |

Fees may vary depending on the service selected.

---

# Passport Processing Time

Processing time depends on the application type.

* **Normal passport:** 30–45 days
* **Tatkal passport:** 7–14 days

Processing time may vary depending on **police verification and document verification**.

---

# How to Track Passport Application Status

Applicants can track their passport application online.

**Step 1:** Visit the Passport Seva Portal:
https://www.passportindia.gov.in/

**Step 2:** Click **“Track Application Status.”**

**Step 3:** Enter your **Application Reference Number (ARN)** or **file number**.

**Step 4:** View the current status of your passport application.

---

# What to Do If Passport Appointment Slots Are Not Available

If appointment slots are unavailable:

* Check nearby **PSK or POPSK locations**
* Try booking during **non-peak hours**
* Refresh the portal periodically
* Consider applying through the **Tatkal service** if urgent
* Contact the **Passport Seva helpline: 1800-258-1800**

---

# Summary

A passport is an essential document for international travel and identity verification. The Indian government has simplified the application process through the **Passport Seva Portal**, allowing citizens to apply online, schedule appointments, and track application status easily.

Applicants can choose between **online or offline application methods**, ensuring flexibility and accessibility for passport services across India.
