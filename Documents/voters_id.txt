# How to Apply for Voter ID Card in Kerala (Online & Offline)

Residents of Kerala can apply for a **Voter ID card** by submitting **Form 6** to the Election Commission of India. After the application is approved, applicants can download their **digital Voter ID (e-EPIC)** through the National Voters’ Service Portal.

Official Portal:
https://www.nvsp.in/

---

# Key Highlights

* **Online Application:** Submit **Form 6** through the National Voters’ Service Portal (NVSP).
* **Offline Application:** Submit the application at the local **Electoral Registration Office**.
* **Documents Required:** Proof of age and proof of residence must be provided.
* **Status Tracking:** Application progress can be tracked using the **application reference number**.

---

# Eligibility to Apply for Voter ID in Kerala

To apply for a Voter ID card in Kerala, the applicant must:

* Be a **citizen of India**
* Be **18 years or older** on the qualifying date
* Be an **ordinary resident of Kerala**
* **Not be registered as a voter in another constituency**

---

# Documents Required for Voter ID Registration

Applicants must provide valid documents during registration.

---

## 1. Age Proof (Any One)

* Birth certificate issued by a local authority or registrar
* Aadhaar card
* PAN card
* Driving licence
* Class X or XII certificate (CBSE/ICSE/State Board)
* Indian passport
* Any other valid document containing date of birth

---

## 2. Address Proof (Any One)

The address proof can be in the applicant’s name or in the name of a **parent, spouse, or adult family member** living at the same address.

Examples include:

* Electricity, water, or gas bill
* Aadhaar card
* Bank or post office passbook
* Indian passport
* Land ownership records
* Registered rent agreement
* Registered sale deed

---

## 3. Photograph

* Recent **passport-size photograph**

All documents should clearly display the applicant’s details.

---

# Steps to Apply for Voter ID Online in Kerala

Residents can apply online using the **National Voters’ Service Portal (NVSP)**.

---

## Apply via NVSP Portal

Official Portal:
https://www.nvsp.in/

**Step 1:** Visit the NVSP portal:
https://www.nvsp.in/

**Step 2:** Register or log in using your **mobile number and OTP**.

**Step 3:** Select **“New Voter Registration (Form 6)”**.

**Step 4:** Enter your:

* Personal details
* Residential address
* Family information

**Step 5:** Upload the required **age proof and address proof documents**.

**Step 6:** Submit the application.

After submission, you will receive a **reference number** which can be used to track your application status.

---

# Apply Through CEO Kerala Website

Residents can also access voter services through the **Chief Electoral Officer (CEO) Kerala portal**.

Official Portal:
https://ceo.kerala.gov.in/

**Step 1:** Visit the CEO Kerala website.

**Step 2:** Click on the **“Voter’s Portal”** or voter services section.

**Step 3:** You will be redirected to the **NVSP portal** to complete the Form 6 application process.

---

# How to Apply for Voter ID Offline in Kerala

Applicants can also submit the application physically.

**Step 1:** Obtain **Form 6** from the local **Electoral Registration Office** or download it from the NVSP website.

**Step 2:** Fill out the form with accurate personal and address details.

**Step 3:** Attach **self-attested copies of age and address proof documents**.

**Step 4:** Submit the form at the designated **Electoral Registration Office**.

**Step 5:** Collect the **acknowledgement slip** for tracking the application.

After verification, the applicant’s name will be added to the **electoral roll**, and the Voter ID will be issued.

---

# How to Check Voter ID Application Status

Applicants can track their Voter ID application online.

**Step 1:** Visit the NVSP portal:
https://www.nvsp.in/

**Step 2:** Log in and go to **“Track Application Status.”**

**Step 3:** Enter your **application reference number**.

**Step 4:** View the current status of your application.

---

# How to Download Digital Voter ID (e-EPIC)

Once the application is approved, you can download the digital Voter ID.

**Step 1:** Visit the NVSP portal:
https://www.nvsp.in/

**Step 2:** Log in and go to the **“e-EPIC Download”** section.

**Step 3:** Enter your **EPIC number or application reference number**.

**Step 4:** Click **“Send OTP.”**

**Step 5:** Enter the OTP received on your registered mobile number.

**Step 6:** Click **“Download e-EPIC”** to download the digital Voter ID.

---

# Uses of Voter ID Card

A Voter ID card serves as both a **voting credential and a valid identity document**.

It is commonly used for:

* Voting in **state and national elections**
* **Identity verification** for government services
* **Address proof** for official purposes
* Verification in the **electoral database**

---

# Summary

Residents of Kerala can apply for a Voter ID card either **online through the NVSP portal** or **offline at the Electoral Registration Office**. By submitting **Form 6 with valid documents**, applicants can register as voters. After approval, they can download their **digital Voter ID (e-EPIC)** online.
