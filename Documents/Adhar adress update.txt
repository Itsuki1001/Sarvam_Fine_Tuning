# How to Change Address in Aadhaar Card (Online & Offline)

Updating the address in your Aadhaar card can be done **online through the myAadhaar portal or Aadhaar mobile app**, or **offline by visiting an Aadhaar Seva Kendra or authorised update centre**.

When you submit an address update request online, a **Service Request Number (SRN)** is generated which can be used to track the status. The update is usually processed within **a few days**, but UIDAI allows **up to 30 days**.

---

# Key Highlights

* **HoF-Based Update:** If the address proof is in the **Head of Family’s name**, the update can be done using **Proof of Relationship (POR)** at an Aadhaar centre.
* **Accepted Documents:** UIDAI provides a list of valid documents for **identity, address, date of birth, and relationship proof**.
* **Fee:** ₹75 is charged for a **demographic address update**.

---

# How to Change Address in Aadhaar Card Online

You can update your Aadhaar address online using the **myAadhaar portal** or the **Aadhaar mobile app**.

---

## Method 1: Using the myAadhaar Portal

**Step 1:** Visit the official myAadhaar portal:
https://myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in

Log in using your **Aadhaar number, captcha code, and OTP**.

**Step 2:** After logging in, select the **“Address Update”** option.

**Step 3:** Click **“Update Aadhaar Online.”**

**Step 4:** Choose **“Address”** and click **“Proceed to Update Aadhaar.”**

**Step 5:** Fill in the required details:

* Enter **Care Of (C/O)** – father’s or husband’s name
* Enter the **new address**
* Select the **post office**
* Choose a **valid Proof of Address (POA)**
* Upload the **supporting document**

**Step 6:** Click **Next**, review the details, and proceed to payment.

**Step 7:** Pay the **₹75 fee** and submit the request.

A **Service Request Number (SRN)** will be generated. Save it to track your update status.

---

## Method 2: Using the Aadhaar Mobile App

**Step 1:** Open the **Aadhaar mobile app** on your smartphone.

**Step 2:** Log in using your **Aadhaar credentials** and complete authentication.

**Step 3:** Select the **Address Update** option.

**Step 4:** Enter the **new address exactly as shown in the Proof of Address document**.

**Step 5:** Upload a valid **Proof of Address (POA)** document.

**Step 6:** Pay the **₹75 fee** and submit the request.

You will receive a **Service Request Number (SRN)** for tracking.

---

# How to Change Address in Aadhaar Card Offline

If you cannot update your address online or prefer offline service, visit an **Aadhaar Seva Kendra or authorised Aadhaar update centre**.

**Step 1:** Visit the nearest Aadhaar update centre.

**Step 2:** Fill out the **Aadhaar Update Form**.

**Step 3:** Submit your **Proof of Address document** and complete **biometric authentication**.

**Step 4:** Pay the **₹75 update fee**.

**Step 5:** You will receive an **acknowledgement slip with a URN (Update Request Number)** to track your request.

---

# Documents Required for Aadhaar Address Change

Some commonly accepted **Proof of Address (POA)** documents include:

* Passport
* Bank statement or passbook
* Post Office account statement
* Ration card
* Voter ID
* Disability ID card
* MGNREGA/NREGS Job Card
* Electricity bill (not older than 3 months)
* Water bill (not older than 3 months)
* Gas connection bill (not older than 3 months)
* Telephone landline or postpaid mobile bill (not older than 3 months)
* Broadband bill (not older than 3 months)
* Property tax receipt (not older than 1 year)

You should also keep a **Proof of Identity (POI)** such as **Passport, PAN card, Voter ID, or Ration card**.

---

# Can You Change Aadhaar Address Without Proof?

No. Aadhaar address cannot be updated **without a valid Proof of Address (POA)**.

The document must contain the **new address** and should either be:

* in **your name**, or
* in the **Head of Family’s name with Proof of Relationship**.

The earlier **Address Validation Letter method has been discontinued**.

# Aadhaar Address Update Fees

| Service                           | Fee                          |
| --------------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Demographic update (Address only) | ₹75                          |
| Biometric update                  | ₹125                         |
| Address + biometric update        | Only biometric fee may apply |

---

# How Long Does Aadhaar Address Update Take?

UIDAI states that the maximum processing time is **30 days**, although most requests are **approved or rejected within a few days**.

---

# How to Check Aadhaar Address Update Status

After submitting the request, you will receive an **SRN (Service Request Number)**.

**Step 1:** Visit the **Aadhaar Update Status page** on the UIDAI portal.

**Step 2:** Enter the **SRN and captcha code**.

**Step 3:** Click **Submit** to view the update status.

---

# How to Download Aadhaar After Address Update

Once the address update is approved, you can download the **updated e-Aadhaar** from the UIDAI portal.

You can download it using:

* Aadhaar Number
* VID (Virtual ID)
* SID
* EID

A **registered mobile number is required for OTP authentication**, and the **e-Aadhaar is digitally signed and legally valid as proof of identity and address**.
